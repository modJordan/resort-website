About Us:

Escape to a Maldives resort on an island fringed by a turquoise lagoon. Laze in your over water luxury villa, puffer fish and baby reef sharks swimming in crystal-clear waters below. At Monstera Resort, write your own chapter of romance. To maintain the serenity of this island, our exclusive resort caters only to adults, one of the Maldives’ few adults-only resorts. Salute the sun in an ocean-facing villa. Discover blissful Ayurvedic treatments at Balance Wellness Spa. Cruise into the sunset or dine under the stars with sand between your toes. Experience limitless possibilities in this luxury Maldives resort.

    67 over water, ocean and beach villas

    Only 30-minutes by speedboat from Malé International Airport

    Couples’ spa journeys, Cinema under the stars on your own private island, and Dining by Design

    Access to facilities at sister resort Anantara Dhigu
